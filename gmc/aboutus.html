ABOUT US

GMC has "embarked" on a mission to bridge the gap between Theory & Practice. The Value chain comprising of students, institutions and industry needs to be interfaced and brought closer to one another. We aim to "impart" through scientific, online as well as offline based modules for the student community to be employable by brushing and honing their skills. We aim to "enable" the institution across the length & breadth of the state closer to the industry by assisting in pre placement as well as placement activities. We aim to "engage" the industry in identifying the skill sets required across various domains and help in reducing the learning curve for all entry level candidates. A visionary team with experience in IT, Training, Technology, Telecom and entrepreneurial ventures are out to make a statement "Together We Can, Together We Shall"
