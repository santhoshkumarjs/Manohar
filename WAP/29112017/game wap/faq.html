FAQ's

What is Mobile Magic Service?

Dear Sir/Madam, this is an interactive WAP site for the Robi Customers. Using this WAP site, user shall be able to view and download all the updated Games, Wallpaper, Animation, and Video Clips for several categories.

I’m a prepaid customer, can I use this?

Dear Sir/Madam, this service is available for both prepaid and postpaid customers.

How do I subscribe to this service?

Dear Sir/Madam, there are 2 ways you may subscribe. One is through Clicking the subscribe link in the site and follow the instructions.

In both the ways, you need to confirm your subscription after giving your initial consent.

Once you subscribe, you would be eligible to download upto 5 content for FREE everyday.

Any further download on the same day would attract charges as per actual.

Can I download my favourite content without subscription?

Yes. You may download even without subscription. Charging would be on a per content basis. However, we suggest that you subscribe to Mobile magic that will make download every economical.

How much does it cost?

Subscription charge of Tk 2 + VAT + SD per day (Tk 2.44) will be charged from your balance in case of prepaid. In case of postpaid, Tk 2.44 per day for the period of use would be added to your monthly bill.

In case of download without subscription, please refer to the individual content cost in the site.

How can I check my download quota balance for the day?

Dear Sir/Madam, please go to My Accounts and you will get all the details

If I subscribe today, till what date is this valid?

Dear Sir/Madam, once subscribed this service is valid till the day you unsubscribe from this service.

How do I deactivate this service?

Dear Sir/Madam, this service may be deactivated by clicking the unsubscription button on any of the page. Alternately you may SMS STOP MM to 16292
