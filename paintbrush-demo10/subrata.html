About

Subrata Das born in Kolkata and studied a Foundation Course from “College of Visual Arts “ Kolkata. The works of Subrata Das have been very poetic representation. He depicts characters from the Indian Mythology. His current series focuses on the Divine love of Radha Krishna, His canvases have a lot of Flora and Fauna Elements, Flute being a constant motif in his paintings giving a surreal mood. Subrata works are Traditional and ethnic with a touch of the Modern. His medium is Acrylic on Canvas & Mixed medium with semi realistic and expressive features and colours.

Subrata has received GOLD and SILVER Awards from “Avantika International Art Exhibition” and Certificate of Merit from “All India Youth Art Exhibition”. His paintings are in collection of UNESCO, TATA steel ITC Group, Raj Bhavan – Kolkata, Bangladesh High Commission, RPG Enterprises, TAJ Group of Hotels and various corporate houses and private collections in India and abroad.

Solo Exhibition

He has held over ten solo shows in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Gurgaon, Hyderabad, Pune and Kolkata, Dubai, Singapore.

Group Shows And Exhibition

And several prestigious groups show in India and abroad. Participated Annual Shows of Birla Academy

  • Academy of Fine Arts
  • AIFACS, National Exhibition
  • Avantika International Art Exhibition
  • Bengal Art Festival in LONDON, etc.
