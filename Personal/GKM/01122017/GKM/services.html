Food

Our food is inspired by traditional Italian cooking, where the best ingredients are sourced and elevated to the next level. Everything is cooked daily, made using fresh, seasonal, high quality ingredients from local, humane, ecological and organic farms as much as possible.

Drinks

Our beverage selection offers seasonal house made sodas, local and Italian craft beers, natural wines, craft cocktails and espresso coffee. Our wine list highlights the uniqueness and diversity of Italy’s terroir, focusing on wines made with a zero intervention approach, respecting the land and showcasing indigenous grape varieties.

Catering

Treat your guests to a delicious Italian meal. We can customize a unique menu from freshly made paninis and salads to pastas and desserts. Available for pick up or delivery.

Private Events
