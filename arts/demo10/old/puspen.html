About

Puspen Niyogi born in 1967 from Hooghly, West Bengal is a Graduated from Rabindra Bharati University, Kolkata. His paintings speak about the unexplained and unfound beauty of the objects. If an art can sing then his painting would do the singing of secrecy in beauty. According to him abstract art is the invisible soul in a painting which turns colours alive. He wants to rediscover the beauty in its most uncertain shapes. Unveiling the unexplored is the journey that he wanted to walk through.
