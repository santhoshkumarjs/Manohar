About

Dewashish Das a young painter, born in Bihar at 1970 is a B.V.A. graduate from Rabindra Bharati University Kolkata in 1995. His painting’s is an observation of nature and life.His paintings, which are done mostly in acrylic on canvas, beside other mediums are inspired by nature. He takes human being as part of nature and feminine beauty as its highest expression.

He place this beauty in his paintings against other elements of nature, flower, creepers or some docile lyrical creatures and set a mute dialogue between them.This is help his to generate a kind of classical sobriety. Decorativeness is an essential ele4ments of folk and tribal art, high art, despite its stress on some essential revealing content.

Delectable or otherwise, very often resorts to this decorative element to infuse a kind of luminal space within the observed reality. Reality is thus gets transcended towards a divine super reality.

His search is for an idealized beauty based on life, festival in moods of life, style and nature.
