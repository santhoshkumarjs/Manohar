Puspen Niyogi

Profile

Abhijit has a unique relation with colors and he started playing with them at a very young age. Owing to his migration to different places one can see the effect of time and travel in his works. In his liners and abstracts one can see the natural essence. Being a trained musician he listens while working and is engrossed in a different world which he expresses in his paintings. He creates joyous moments of his life on the canvas which he enjoys. As a spontaneous person who is always motivated by his own instinct, his works are free from the burden of subjectivity.
