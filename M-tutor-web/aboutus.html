Singh Ratnam,

Director

Singh is a Chartered Accountant by profession with a diverse background in business. He started his career in the corporate sector across various reputed industries working in the field of finance. His panache for marketing helped establish Chennai Jewellers as a major player in the U.A.E. His deep-rooted expertise in finance and knowledge in business will enhance M-Tutor’s vision and expansion in the future. His hobbies include taking road trips across the country.

Priya Rachel Singh,

Director

Priya is a dynamic entrepreneur who developed strong business acumen from a very young age. Hailing from a business background, she developed her prowess in jewellery design as she worked with design houses around the world. Her avid interest towards technology and innovation has led to her collaboration with M-tutor; an association that is sure to take things to the next level. In her free time, she is out shopping or spending time with her dog.

V. Sundaramoorthy,

Co-founder CEO

Since starting M-Tutor, Sundar has found it incredibly fun to build it into a powerful learning tool, that’s currently used by millions of students worldwide. As a team, they're also trying to be instrumental in significantly shaping their educational transformation, and thus constantly researching on its various parameters. He spends a lot of his time thinking about the company’s growth, work culture, team building, engineering, and customer happiness. Aside his love for travel, he also helps other startup founders, and dabbles with new ideas and innovations.

T. Padmakumar,

Co-founder CTO

Padma believes that the dynamics of education are constantly changing, and philosophizes in creating products that address this ever-growing trend through constant innovation. Thus, came about his role as the CTO when he and Sundar co-founded M-Tutor. With his love for technology, he’s pushing boundaries and propelling growth, while working closely with the design and technology teams to support the evolution of the product. Among other things, swimming is something that relaxes him, and serves as a source of soulful fuel.
